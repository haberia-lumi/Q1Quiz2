Tangled

There once was a pregnant Queen who became sick. The kingdom searched for a cure, which was the magic golden flower . This flower healed the queen and she gave birth to a healthy, golden-haired baby girl. Mother Gothel found Rapunzel and kidnapped her because of her magical abilities. She was locked in a tower for 18 years, until she met Flynn Rider. He helped her escape from the tower and reunited her with her parents.


Setting

Tangled is set in the Kingdom of Corona.
